About

WarioCools Vs Nazis [WVN] is an action packed 3D game that can be ported to anything like a school computer, smart fridge, Vision Pro and eventually even the NeuralLink and whatever isn't ancient, and your teachers computer!

Plot

Goal of the game is to shoot and kill nazis, however your not in nazi Germany, your in stargate and you've went through a strange worm whole of 2024 and nazi Germany all in one creating a weird timelime, the main amazing epic main protagonist that gets revealed at the end of the game must escape from stargate and kill all the evil nazis!!!

Multiplayer

WVN also includes a very real online mode to the game, well this part of the game is still under development, but once it's done you'll be able to chat, talk with your headsets, invite friends, ooooh it'll be so much fun!!!!!

Storyline

The very real main protagonist who is actually really amazing and cool ends up in a weird twisted stargate dimension, he/she questions if all this is even REAL or if it's UNREAL, it starts with demons and monsters trying to kill them till they discover that they are not alone, they defeat the bosses incharge of this twisted dimension all to find they were lost in it and the entire thing was a trap or not, play NOW!!!
